Simple Calculator

A simple calculator, often referred to as a basic or standard calculator, is a device or application designed for performing essential mathematical operations. These operations typically include addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division. Simple calculators provide a user-friendly interface with numeric buttons, arithmetic operators, and an equal sign (=) to calculate and display results quickly. They are commonly used for everyday arithmetic calculations and are handy tools for tasks that require basic mathematical functions.

To-Do List

A to-do list is a simple and effective task management tool that helps individuals organize and prioritize their daily or long-term tasks. It typically consists of a list of items or activities that need to be completed, accompanied by checkboxes or other visual markers to indicate their status. To-do lists are valuable for improving productivity and time management, ensuring that important tasks are not forgotten, and offering a sense of accomplishment as items are checked off when completed. They can be created on paper, digitally in apps or software, or through a combination of both methods, allowing individuals to tailor their lists to their specific needs and preferences.
